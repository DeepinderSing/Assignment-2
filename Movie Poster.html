Shutter Island

Description: In the year 1954, U.S. Marshal Edward "Teddy" Daniels and his partner Chuck Aule investigate the disappearance of a patient named Rachel Solando at Ashecliffe Hospital for the criminally insane on Shutter Island. Teddy, haunted by his wartime experiences and the death of his wife Dolores, suspects a conspiracy. As the investigation unfolds, Teddy becomes increasingly paranoid, encountering mysterious events and questioning the reality of his surroundings. However, a twist reveals that Teddy is actually Andrew Laeddis, a patient at the hospital who created a false identity to cope with the guilt of murdering his wife. The entire investigation, including Chuck's existence, was a psychological role-play designed by the hospital staff to help Andrew confront his true identity. In the end, Andrew willingly accepts a lobotomy as a form of escape, posing the poignant question of whether it is better "to live as a monster or to die as a good man."

Genre: Mystry . Thriller

Director: Martin Scorsese

Writer: Laeta Kalogridis . Dennis Lehane

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio . Emily Mortimer. Mark Ruffalo
